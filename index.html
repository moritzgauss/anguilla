The Anguilla Lamp

An innovative lamp with a low-voltage lighting system and sleek modern design.

Innovative Low-Voltage Lighting

This lamp is a prime example of a low-voltage lighting system, operating without conventional wiring to the light source. The 12V current is conducted through the metallic frame, completely eliminating the need for external electrical cables. The lamp’s frame is made entirely of conductive metal, providing an elegant and minimalist design solution.

It uses a GU10 to GU5.3 adapter with a GU5.3 LED and an LED transformer to convert 230V to 12V. Made from flat steel (1.5mm) and aluminum sheet (2mm), the lamp achieves a sleek, industrial yet modern look that fits perfectly into any space.

Embossed Metal Design

  • Conductive metal frame
  • GU10 to GU5.3 adapter
  • LED Transformer (230V to 12V)
